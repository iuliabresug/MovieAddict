How to Train Your Dragon (2010)
Genre: Animation, Adventure, Family 
Directors: Dean DeBlois, Chris Sanders
Writers: William Davies (screenplay) (as Will Davies), Dean DeBlois (screenplay)
Stars: Jay Baruchel, Gerard Butler, Christopher Mintz-Plasse
A hapless young Viking who aspires to hunt dragons, becomes the unlikely friend of a young dragon himself, 
and learns there may be more to the creatures than he assumed.
