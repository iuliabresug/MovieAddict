12 Angry Men (1957)
Genre:	Drama
Director: Sidney Lumet
Writers: Reginald Rose (story), Reginald Rose (screenplay)
Stars: Henry Fonda, Lee J. Cobb, Martin Balsam 
A jury argues a case in a stuffy room on a hot summer's day. Eleven say "guilty!" 
But one holdout (Jack Lemmon) is convinced of the defendant's innocence and stubbornly argues "reasonable doubt."
