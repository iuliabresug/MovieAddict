Black Swan (2010)
Genre: Drama, Thriller 
Director: Darren Aronofsky
Writers: Mark Heyman (screenplay), Andres Heinz (screenplay) (as Andrés Heinz)
Stars: Natalie Portman, Mila Kunis, Vincent Cassel
A psychological thriller set in the world of New York City ballet, BLACK SWAN stars Natalie Portman as Nina, 
a featured dancer who finds herself locked in a web of competitive intrigue with a new rival at the company (Mila Kunis).
