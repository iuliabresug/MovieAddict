Singin' in the Rain (1952)
Genre: Comedy, Musical, Romance
Director: Stanley Donen, Gene Kelly
Writers: Adolph Green (story), Betty Comden (story)
Stars: Gene Kelly, Donald O'Connor, Debbie Reynolds
Gene Kelly, Debbie Reynolds and Donald O'Connor star in Singin' in the Rain, one of the greatest and most successful
musicals ever filmed - filled with memorable songs, lavish routines and Kelly's fabulous song-and-dance number 
performed in the rain.
