Vicky Cristina Barcelona (2008)
Director: Woody Allen
Writer: Woody Allen
Stars: Rebecca Hall, Scarlett Johansson, Javier Bardem 
Vicky and Cristina, these two young Americans spend a summer in Spain and meet a flamboyant artist and his beautiful 
but insane ex-wife. Vicky is straight-laced and about to be married. Cristina is a sexually adventurous free spirit.
