Gia (1998)
Genre:  Biography, Drama, Romance 
Director: Michael Cristofer
Writers: Jay McInerney, Michael Cristofer
Stars: Angelina Jolie, Faye Dunaway, Elizabeth Mitchell
Angelina Jolie (Wallace) has the title role in this biographical drama tracing the tragic rise to fame and death, at age 26, of insecure, self-destructive supermodel Gia Carangi, who grew up in Philadelphia a victim of child abuse and moved to New York to work as a model in 1977.
