Saving Private Ryan (1998)
Genre: Action, Drama, War 
Director: Steven Spielberg
Writer: Robert Rodat
Stars: Tom Hanks, Matt Damon, Tom Sizemore

Steven Spielberg directed this powerful, realistic re-creation of WWII's D-day invasion and the immediate aftermath. 
The story opens with a prologue in which a veteran brings his family to the American cemetery at Normandy, 
and a flashback then joins Capt. John Miller (Tom Hanks) and GIs in a landing craft making the June 6, 1944, 
approach to Omaha Beach to face devastating German artillery fire.
